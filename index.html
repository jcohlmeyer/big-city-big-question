About this Project

Toronto is by far the busiest city in Canada. It is the most multicultural city in the world, with people of different ethnicities, socioeconomic backgrounds, career goals, and religious beliefs. Unfortunately, the busy city lifestyle and our rigid schedules can often distract us from giving attention to the things that make life truly meaningful.

This campaign seeks to penetrate the superficial dialogue taking place in Toronto. What is the taxi driver’s biggest fear? What are the ambitions of your co-workers? What is the person on the street corner passionate about? These are the questions we hope to ask and further investigate. Follow us as we explore the streets of Toronto and discover the questions that fill the minds of people in this city.
